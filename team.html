Team Rules


  • Behavior: All team members should notify the others/client if they will be late or will miss a scheduled meeting. Team communications will take place mainly on Slack, with discussions on scheduling, design, and development focused on appropriate channels. We will aim to meet weekly to discuss the direction of development with the client, outline our development targets for the next week, and pair program with each other. Our goal is to have every member understand each part of the code so everyone can collaborate and help with each other's problems. Lastly, all of our design decisions will be made with end-user in mind.

  • Style: We will be following the coding style guidelines provided by Airbnb for Javascript and React.
