Understanding the Trauma Ready mobile app

Trauma Ready is a mobile app that was created for the Trauma Department at UNC Hospitals by a group of computer science students at UNC. It runs on both iPhone and Android, but is currently only available as an alpha version on the Google Play store (i.e. Android). Its purpose is to provide a pocket-reference for all the information that you as a paramedic might need to route patients to different hospitals.
